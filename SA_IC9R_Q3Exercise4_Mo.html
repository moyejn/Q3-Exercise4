Boracay

A tropical island called Boracay is located in the Philippines, roughly an hour's flight from Metro Manila. It is one of the most popular tourist sites in the nation and offers excellent long white sand beaches. Boracay is known for its tiny islands and immaculate white sand beaches. The island is home to a variety of aquatic life, lush flora and wildlife, and pure white sand. This is the reason why Boracay's White Beach has earned recognition as one of the world's most stunning beaches throughout the years. Boracay gives you a variety of activities that ensure a well-deserved holiday, whether you're taking a short break from the everyday stress of city life or staying for a longer get-away trip.
